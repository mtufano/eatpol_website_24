Frequently Asked Questions

How often will I be asked to test food?

Testing frequency depends on your location and our current projects. Most testers participate in 1-3 tests per month.

How much will I get paid?

Compensation varies by test complexity, but typically ranges from €10-€20 per test, plus you get to enjoy free food!

What happens to my video recording?

Your privacy is our priority. We use AI facial masking technology to anonymize your identity while still capturing your reactions. Videos are analyzed by our AI and securely stored according to GDPR regulations.

Do I need any special equipment?

Just a smartphone with a camera and internet connection. We'll provide simple instructions for setting up your recording.
