How It Works

AI-Driven Consumer Testing

You prepare your food samples 🍱—we handle the rest. Eatpol ships them to testers along with a QR code 📦. Testers cook and eat while recording themselves 🎥, with privacy ensured via AI facial masking.

Our AI software analyzes how consumers interact with your samples in real-life settings 🏠. You'll receive a detailed report featuring:

  • 📊 Traditional product questionnaire results (liking, taste, purchase intent, etc.)
  • 😊 Emotion detection, ❤️ heart rate, and activity tracking
  • 🍽️ Bite & chew counts per sample for insights on eating behavior
  • 🔊 Audio feedback interpreted by AI to capture nuances beyond surveys

Get a complete picture of how consumers use, react to, eat, and prefer your samples—compared to the competition 🥇.
