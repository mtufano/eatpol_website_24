The old way is broken. Products are tested in sensory boothssterile, artificial settings that ignore real life.

In these booths, testers aren't cooking dinner for their family; they are "evaluating." They give rational feedback, take their payment, and leave.

Real food choices happen in chaos, in kitchens, with emotions. If you aren't testing there, you aren't really testing.

FAQs

How is privacy handled?

Faces are automasked in videos; we store deidentified signals only and comply with GDPR. A Data Processing Agreement (DPA) is available on request.

Do we need special hardware?

Notesters use their own phones. We provide simple setup guidance inside the app.

How fast are results?

Typical studies deliver a report within 7-10 days after the last test is performed. Timelines depend on audience and logistics.

Can we compare against competitors?

Yesinclude one or multiple competitor SKUs. We report relative performance and key drivers.

What if samples require cooking?

We capture preparation steps to connect outcomes with realworld usage and identify friction.
