About Eatpol

Eatpol is a software developed at Wageningen University and Research with the aim to innovate eating behavior research and food development.
The demo below shows how Eatpol counts the number and duration of bites, chews, and sips from a recorded video while preserving privacy thanks to facial recognition and AI.
