2. Service Description

Eatpol operates as an intermediary platform connecting food companies with consumers for product testing purposes. We facilitate:

  • Distribution of food samples from third-party companies to testers
  • Collection of consumer feedback and reactions
  • Compensation processing for completed tests
  • AI-powered analysis of tester responses

Important: Eatpol acts solely as a facilitator and intermediary. We do not manufacture, prepare, or control the food products being tested.

3. Tester Responsibilities

3.1 Eligibility

  • Tester must be 18 years or older
  • Must have access to a smartphone with camera and internet connection
  • Must be able to consume food products (dietary restrictions can be noted during registration)

3.2 Testing Process

  • Follow all provided instructions for food preparation and consumption
  • Record yourself consuming the product as directed
  • Provide honest, accurate feedback via audio, video, or questionnaires
  • Complete tests within specified timeframes
  • Maintain confidentiality of unreleased products

3.3 Health and Safety

  • You are solely responsible for your own health and safety
  • Review all ingredient lists and allergen information before consumption
  • Do not consume products if you have known allergies or medical conditions that could be affected
  • Discontinue consumption immediately if you experience adverse reactions
  • Seek medical attention if needed and notify Eatpol of any incidents
