For Each Product: 3 Videos

Video 1: OpeningWhen you first open the package

  • Show how easy or difficult it is to open
  • Share your first impressions (look, smell)
  • Note anything surprising

Video 2: EatingWhen you prepare and taste it

  • Show how you prepare or serve it
  • Describe the taste and texture
  • Share what you like or dislike

Video 3: StoringWhen you store what's left

  • Show where and how you store it
  • Note if packaging reseals easily
  • Mention any storage concerns
